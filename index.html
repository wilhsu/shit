The Sh!t Zine is a collective of five bookmakers, screenprinters, and riso enthusiasts who see beauty in chaos, celebrate the unexpected, and embrace the messiness of creativity. Inspired by the intimate act of shitting, our process is a continuously iterative one, where we individually draw inspiration from a myriad of sources, assimilate it together, and collectively produce new work, discarding whatever doesn’t resonate along the way.
